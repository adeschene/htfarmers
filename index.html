HomelessToFarmers is on a mission to eliminate homelessness and poverty.

HomelessToFarmers is a nonprofit, aiming to create businesses, and thus create opportunities, and use our profits to put end to homelessness, poverty, and build a better and safer society for ourselves and our children.
